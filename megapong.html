Web Mega Pong - Javascript Version

Copyright (c)2008, Jean-Christophe Hoelt (jeko@free.fr)


Do not let the balls explode on your territory. The biggest the ball, the biggest the points!

Controls:

UP and DOWN to move vertically.

CTRL to shoot the ball.

Compatibility

Made with CAKE, so it should only works on browsers that have canvas support (Firefox 2+, Safari, Opera 9.5, ...)

Tested only on: Firefox 2 and Epiphany
